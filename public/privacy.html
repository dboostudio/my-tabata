Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Overview

MyTabata ("we", "the app") is a free Tabata interval timer available at tabata.my. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how data is handled.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information. The app does not require registration, login, or any personal data to function.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device (via browser localStorage). This data never leaves your device:

  • Workout history (date, duration, rounds, intervals)
  • App settings (language, theme, timer configuration)
  • Custom presets

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand general usage patterns. GA4 may collect:

  • Anonymous usage events (workout start/complete, feature usage)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Approximate geographic region

No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected. You can opt out by using browser privacy settings or ad blockers.

Advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties, except as described above (Google Analytics and AdSense).

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
